#  PROJECTS

These are some of the most important projects and experiences I had. Some where conducted at the University of Pittburgh, and others were professional experiences. Video Demos and more are provided!

  * Data Science Project: This project was conducted during STAT1261 (Principles of Data Science), where I studied the effect of several factors on deaths by heart failures alongside 2 teammates. We used multiple Data Visualization and Wrangling techniques in R, and trained several Machine Learning algorithms to find the optimal accuracy.
    
  * Database Management System: This is part of my professinal experience at College Louise Wegmann (Beirut, Lebanon) as a Freelance Software Developer. This is just a snippet of what I worked on. I am not able to provide the rest of the codes and deliverables due to accessibility and privacy reasons. The provided material include the SQL diagrams and codes, which I completed alongisde other colleagues.

  *  Dispose It: This project was conducted during ECE1895 (Junior Design). I worked with 2 teammates to design and build a "Bop-It" inspired game called "Dispose It". The device prompts several items that the user has to dispose of correctly by triggering one of three sensors corresponding to the following actions: "Recycle It", "Trash It", and "Compost It". I designed the Hardware unit of the system (which is the part provided in the "Material" folder). Video Demo provided.

  *  Facial Recognition Lock System: This project was conducted during ECE1896 (Senior Design). I worked with 2 teammates to build a device that can perform facial recognition with high accuracies. I worked on the Machine Learning unit, which envolved building the facial recognition system using a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) as well as devising an Anti-Spoofing system based on blink detection to prevent fraudulent attempts to break in (by placing a picture of the authorized user in front of the camera). I used libraries such as Tensorflow, Keras, and Scikit-Learn in Python. All the codes and supporting materials are provided. Video Demo provided.

  *  Research at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK): This project was conducted during a Summer Undergraduate Research Program where I researched Robotics used for biomedical purposes, as well as Deep Learning algorithms to localize targetted areas in soft tissues. The final paper (also published on MDPI) is provided, along with all the references used.

  *  Train Control System: This project was conducted during ECE1140 (Project Management). I worked in a team of 6 on a train control system, and was responsible for the Wayside/Track Controller (Software). I controlled traffic lights, railway crossings, switch positions, and detected power & circuit failures. A demo is provided, along with all the supporting documents and code (please view the folder "Track_Controller_Software" to view my own code). Some libraries used are PyQt for UI design, and unittest for Unit Testing, both in Python. Video Demo provided.
